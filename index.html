To my love...
Dear Football,
You taught me Geography. You taught me Friendship. You taught me how to love a club in their defeats and winnings. You taught me love hurts but I cant leave your love. You kept me awake at nights and the next day I woke up early to play football.I bunked classes to play football. Now I am getting old. I have stopped staying awake to watch you mesmerize me with those free kicks and bicycle kicks. I am so afraid of everything now. So afraid that my team "Manchester United" will lose that I dont even care about them winning or losing. It hurts me when people say so many things about my club. I am a fan,I don't get money for my support, I can't even go to Old Trafford to watch them play. I dont get time to watch you play. I am always thinking why should I watch you as it is not helping me in any way. This are hard times, I have no idea what the future holds. But I don't want to loose you. Whatever may happen. Just promise me football that you will never change. Even if I start to hate you, you will always have a place in my heart. Those UEFA Champions League and FIFA World Cup nights I will never forget them. Thankyou for teaching me where Ecuador is! And for teching me how to speak Italian. Thankyou!!!
Yours faithfully,
A Broken Fan
